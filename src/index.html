About me

Teaghan, pronounced ‘Tay’ instead of ‘Tea’ is a 3D animation student from Mohawk College. With a love for all things cute and colourful, Teaghan’s work consists of bright and quirky creations.

After completing an independent short film and currently working on her second, Teaghan enjoys creating endearing stories with vibrant aesthetics akin to the cartoons she had grown up with. Specializing in animation and modeling, Teaghan strives use her passion and knowledge of animation to create something for all families to enjoy.
